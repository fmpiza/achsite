Welcome Note

Dr. Salma Kitara, Medical Officer In- Charge (MOI)

Arusha City Hospital was first established and opened on 17 October, 2021 by Hon. President Samia S. Hassan, as the declaration of United Republic of Tanzania (U.R.T) with the aim of ensuring the stability of health services provision under the Arusha City Council.


Arusha City Hospital was established to make sure the provision of Delivery Pack services, Both Communicable and Non Communicable Diseases are well being controlled and prevented by using our estemeed health specialists


Arusha City Hospital consist of 4+ Private Wards and more than 10 Public Wards for the Patients who get services in the hospital


Also, Arusha City Hospital gives services to around 1000+ People available in and out of Arusha City Council by their kinds of treatments including Out-Patient, In-Patients and other patients categories, CHF patients as well as patients under Exemptions
